BENDS

Welcome to Bends!

An intereactive demonstration of how massive objects warp and bend space-time. A departure from typical, 2-dimensional displays of this phenomenon -- Bends is an entirely 3-dimensional experience for all you 3D beings!

Use the directional sliders to move the sphere through the box.

Drag the box to get sweet views of its distorted figure.

Zoom in and out at your own risk!
